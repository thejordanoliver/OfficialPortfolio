    About Me

    Digital Media BA

    I’m Jordan Oliver, a creative thinker and web designer passionate about innovation, accessibility, and user engagement. I graduated from the University of Central Florida in the Spring of 2025. I specialize in UI/UX design, front-end development, and digital experiences that merge functionality with creativity.

    My background spans visual design, digital marketing, and API development, with experience in tools like Figma, React Native, Framer, and PostgreSQL. I’ve worked on projects ranging from sports app development to event planning platforms and even alternative gaming controllers. I focus on crafting intuitive, visually compelling designs that enhance user interactions.

    Beyond design, I have a deep appreciation for sports, gaming, and sneaker culture, and I love exploring how technology can elevate these experiences. Whether I’m refining an interface, coding an interactive feature, or brainstorming innovative solutions, my goal is always to create seamless, engaging digital experiences that leave an impact.
